RECIPES

FAWORKI

Faworki, also known as Crust or Angel Wings, is a delightful, crispy light dough snack, which has been deeply fried in oil and topped with sweet powdered sugar. The time to prepare these takes around 30 minutes and the process is very simple, just follow the intrtuctions below.


INGREDIENTS

  • 400g (2cups) of flour
  • 1 tablespoon of sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 egg
  • 4 egg yokes
  • 3-5 tablespoons of sour cream
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla or citrus zest (optional)
  • 2L of lard or oil for frying
  • powdered sugar

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Combine flour, sugar & salt in a relatively big bowl
  2. In a seperate bowl, combine the eggs, sour cream, and vanilla or citrus zest if present
  3. Add both ingredients together into the big bowl and stir until the dough comes together, add more milk or sour cream if necessary
  4. Place dough on the table which has been powdered with flour before-hand so that the dough wouldn't stick to the surface and roll out to a very thin state
  5. Cut the dough into rectangular pieces, around 1x3 inches or so, with a small slit in the center
  6. Pull one end of the rectangle through the slit and twist
  7. Heat lard/oil to approx 160-180 degrees Celsius
  8. Fry in batches so that each has its own space, approx. 7-12 each time
  9. Take out when cripsy golden and place onto paper towels and allow to dry for about 30 seconds
  10. Place in a bowl and top with powdered sugar
